The Atlas BootCamp exists to guide motivated individuals toward becoming competent website developers. By following this program you will be able to build an impressive resume and portfolio.

Where to Start

To become a developer, you must first learn to think like a developer. Your first step is to take this bucket and mop, and begin cleaning my kitchen floor.

When you're finished, you'll need to have a basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A lot of helpful guides can be found on YouTube, but you'll need to purchase a few Udemy courses. Don't pay full price, they often go on sale for around 80% off. Here are a few resources to get started:
